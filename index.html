About Us

At Shri Sahyadri Agro Multistate Cooperative Society Ltd., our mission is to support farmers with the essentials they need to thrive—quality seeds, effective fertilizers, and reliable pesticides. We’re dedicated to enhancing farm productivity, crop health, and long-term soil sustainability through trusted agro solutions and farmer-first service.

What We Offer

We offer high-yield seeds, nutrient-rich fertilizers, and trusted pesticides—empowering farmers to grow healthier crops, achieve stronger yields, and embrace sustainable farming.
